About Us

Welcome to EzeKeb Mobility, your Ezekeb is revolutionizing transportation with a pioneering electric cab service in Rajasthan. As an extension of Ezecon Build Projects, a leader in renewable energy and EV infrastructure, we offer a first-of-its-kind EV fleet supported by in-house charging. Our mission is to provide eco-friendly, reliable, and affordable rides that reduce carbon footprints and promote sustainable urban mobility. With a customer-first approach, cutting-edge technology, and a focus on green practices, Ezekeb is driving change for a cleaner, greener future—one electric ride at a time.

With EzeKeb Mobility, enjoy flexible payment options, 24/7 support, and seamless booking at your fingertips. Join us and experience the journey of convenience and comfort.
